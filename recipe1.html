Spaghetti Carbonara

Home Spaghetti Carbonara Chicken Curry Pancakes Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and mix with the pancetta and egg mixture.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and serve immediately.
