Chicken Curry

Home Spaghetti Carbonara Chicken Curry Pancakes Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken breast
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Saute the onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add the chicken and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in the curry powder and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the coconut milk, bring to a boil, then simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and serve with rice.
