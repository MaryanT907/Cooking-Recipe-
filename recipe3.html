Ingredients

  • 200g plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Butter for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in the eggs and milk until smooth.
  3. Heat a little butter in a frying pan and pour in some batter.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side.
  5. Serve with syrup, fruit, or your favorite toppings.
