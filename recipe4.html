Caesar Salad

Home Spaghetti Carbonara Chicken Curry Pancakes Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 romaine lettuce
  • 50g croutons
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing
  • Grilled chicken (optional)

Instructions

  1. Chop the lettuce and place in a large bowl.
  2. Add croutons and grated Parmesan cheese.
  3. Toss with Caesar dressing until well coated.
  4. Top with grilled chicken if desired, and serve.
